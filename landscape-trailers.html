Frequently Asked Questions

What is a landscape trailer?

A landscape trailer is purpose-built for lawn care and landscaping businesses, featuring high mesh or solid sides, ramp gates, and often racks for weed eaters and trimmers.

How much does a landscape trailer cost?

Landscape trailer prices vary by size and features. We offer affordable options for startup landscaping businesses. Call (361) 387-9108 for pricing.

What size landscape trailer do I need?

Most lawn care businesses start with a 6x12 or 7x14 landscape trailer. As your business grows, a larger tandem axle model provides more capacity.

Can you add custom racks and storage to a landscape trailer?

Yes! We customize landscape trailers with weed eater racks, trimmer traps, tool boxes, and other storage options.
