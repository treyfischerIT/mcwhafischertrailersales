Heavy-Duty Gooseneck Trailers for Ranch, Farm & Construction

McWha Fischer Trailer Sales carries a full lineup of gooseneck lowboy and deckover trailers for sale in Corpus Christi and Robstown, TX. When you need to haul heavy equipment, machinery, or oversized loads, a gooseneck trailer delivers the payload capacity and towing stability that a standard bumper pull cannot match.

Our gooseneck trailers feature a hitch that mounts in the bed of your pickup truck, placing the tongue weight directly over the rear axle. This design provides superior towing stability, tighter turning radius, and significantly higher payload capacity compared to conventional trailers. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a gooseneck lowboy and deckover?

A lowboy has a lower deck height for easier loading and a lower center of gravity. A deckover sits on top of the frame for maximum deck width and flat loading surface.

How much weight can a gooseneck trailer haul?

Gooseneck trailers typically handle 14,000 to 25,000+ lbs depending on the configuration. We can help you find the right capacity for your needs.

Do I need a gooseneck hitch?

Yes, gooseneck trailers require a gooseneck ball hitch mounted in the bed of your truck. This provides superior stability and turning radius compared to bumper pull.

Do you build custom gooseneck trailers?

Yes, we build custom gooseneck trailers to your specifications including custom lengths, decking, and tie-down configurations.
