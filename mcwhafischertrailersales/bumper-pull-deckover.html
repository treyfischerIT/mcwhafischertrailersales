Frequently Asked Questions

What is a bumper pull deckover trailer?

A bumper pull deckover has the deck sitting on top of the frame, providing a full-width flat loading surface. It connects to a standard ball hitch, so no gooseneck hitch is needed.

How wide is a bumper pull deckover trailer?

Most bumper pull deckover trailers are 102 inches (8.5 feet) wide, providing maximum deck space for hauling equipment and materials.

What can I haul on a bumper pull deckover?

Bumper pull deckovers are versatile -- they're used for equipment, lumber, hay bales, ATVs, and any wide or heavy loads that benefit from a flat deck surface.

How long can a bumper pull deckover be?

We carry bumper pull deckovers from 14ft to 24ft and can custom order longer lengths depending on your needs.
