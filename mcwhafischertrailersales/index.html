About McWha Fischer Trailer Sales

McWha Fischer Trailers is a private, family-owned business operating in Robstown, Texas since 2001. Ernest and Cynthia Fischer started as consignment dealers before growing into a full trailer dealership serving Corpus Christi and the entire Coastal Bend region.

We go beyond being trailer dealers. If you need a minor repair on your trailer, never hesitate to call. We pride ourselves on lifetime customer relationships and after-purchase support.

Whether you need a cargo trailer, utility trailer, or a custom-designed rig, we deliver superior quality at prices that work for your budget.
