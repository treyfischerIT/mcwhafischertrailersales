Flatbed Deckover Trailers Without the Gooseneck

McWha Fischer Trailer Sales carries a versatile selection of bumper pull deckover trailers for sale in Corpus Christi and Robstown, TX. A deckover trailer gives you the full-width flatbed loading surface you need, but with a standard bumper pull hitch -- so there's no need for a gooseneck setup or bed-mounted hitch in your truck.

Our bumper pull flatbed trailers are ideal for hauling wide loads, equipment, pallets, building materials, ATVs, and machinery. The deck-over-axle design means the entire deck is available for cargo, with no wheel wells cutting into your load space. Load from the rear with a ramp or from the sides with ease.
